Look at the stars is an extended image viewer for Linux that combines the easy use of GQview and features from Photogroup and CompuPic. It supports colored backgrounds, image manipulation, and more.

Hello again :) 2002-10-07
Some people asked me for a release of the gnome-2 variant. Well, it's not ready for a release. But I think those who want to know what I'm doing for some time now should have a preview :)

For the impatient:
- grab this archive
- unpack it with tar xzvf lats-2-0.1-20021007.tar.gz
- cd lats-2
- ./configure
- make
- src/lats-2 should start it

Sure, you can also do a make install but it's not necessary - it's a preview - remember! :)
I really hope it works for you... To the right you can also see a first screenshot of it...

Hint.. or nearly a bug fix ;) 2002-07-17
Hi everyone!

If you wonder why you can't open .pdf or .ps files with my baby than you might have an older version of acroread! This problem is around lats/src/imageview.c . There's a left-over of my testing period trying to optimize acroreads reading time :) Just delete this string "-transQuality 5" everywhere you find it, do a "make" and get happy :) If you find any other problems, bugs, suggestions or even wishes for lats - please send me an email!
